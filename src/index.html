01about

I study the arithmetic and geometry of memory and cognition — how periodic structures like grid cells and prefrontal circuits organise thought through attractor dynamics, and how defects in those networks surface as schizophrenia, epilepsy and dementia.

Supervised by Izaak Neri, Daniel Bush and Rick Adams, across the Disordered Systems Group at King's, neuroscience and computational psychiatry at UCL.
